Chocolate Mousse

Description

A rich, chocolatey dessert.

This dish came from France, and is prepared by artfully melting and combining cream and chocolate

Best served cold, with whipped cream on top

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate
  • Cream
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate over a double-boiler, and add cream
  2. Seperate eggs, and whip egg whites to soft peaks. Fold in sugar, and whip till glossy
  3. Fold egg mixture into the cooling choclate mixture
  4. Pour into seperate ramekins, and refrigerate to set
