Noodle Stir Fry

Description

Noodle stir fry is an quick, easy dinner that can be made very simply

You can add meat to this dish, by removing the vegetables from the pan one cooked, and stir-frying your chosen meat

Stir fry is tasty and delicious, and very versatile!

Ingredients

  • Vegatables such as:
    • Bok choy
    • Carrots
    • Celery
    • Broccoli
  • Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Worchester Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook and drain noodles
  2. While noodles are cooking, cut vegetables into small pieces and stir fry
  3. Apply noodle sauces according to taste (2 tablespoons of each)
  4. Mix in noodles and serve hot
